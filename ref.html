This is a small list of external sites that are more specialized in their use that will be helpful when diving into anime.


Crunchyroll This is the most popular (free) streaming platform. Lots of notable anime, some obscure, and others of some popularity are licensed here.
Funimation The website where the English dubbed voice acting are licensed (and also behind a paywall), although Funimation is still merged into Crunchyroll, it however still has certain licenses exclusive to them compared to Crunchyroll.
MyAnimeList The website is a user sourced "database" with its own community. This is my personal go-to if I'm unsure about watching a show and seeing if it's well liked, or reading reviews after I finish a show to see how other people responded to it. As it also lists the voice actors, staff, and studio involved, it's easier to navigate to their specific works if you like a particular person.
