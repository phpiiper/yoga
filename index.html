Introduction

Anime, known internationally to encompass only animation from Japan, is rooted in, well, Japanese culture. Originally having a much more traditional style of older Japanese art, the current style became established in the 1960s.

Reaching wide success during the 1980s, it was in the 2000s with titles like Sailor Moon, Dragon Ball Z, Pokemon, and Studio Ghibli movies like Spirited Away started to gain international attraction. As such, the market expanded ever since internationally.

So, this website is here to get you into anime. It's hard without guidance, so this will be your one-stop guide to learning how to get into anime and how to navigate the community.

You can't go searching around anime without looking for specific keywords. Not only that, but traveling through the anime community without knowing certain parts of it is quite difficult without being taught.
